1. discusses the meanings and measurement of food poverty and food
insecurity.
2. considers historical accounts of the relations between food and poverty.
3. is concerned with research on food and poverty, focusing on early studies of food poverty, work on austerity and food banks, critical perspectives on food poverty, ‘more than food aid’ approaches to food poverty, and rights to food.
